# Experience Sharing After Participating in the First Cloud AI Journey Program

After participating in the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** program, I had the opportunity to gain new knowledge about **Cloud Computing, AWS, AI, and practical system development processes**. The program not only helped me strengthen my technical knowledge but also improved my self-learning ability, problem-solving mindset, and teamwork skills.

Through the lessons, hands-on labs, and guidance from mentors, I gained a better understanding of how Cloud systems are designed, deployed, and operated in real-world environments.

---

# General Evaluation

## 1. Learning and Working Environment

The FCAJ program provides a friendly environment with many favorable conditions for learning and practicing. Members are always willing to discuss and support each other when facing difficulties during hands-on exercises.

What impressed me the most is the spirit of knowledge sharing and teamwork among participants. Everyone actively discussed problems and worked together to find solutions. This helped me feel more comfortable asking questions and proactively exploring new knowledge.

However, the program could organize more networking activities or team-building sessions so that members have more opportunities to connect and understand each other better.

---

## 2. Support from Mentors and Team Admin

During the program, mentors provided enthusiastic support and gave clear explanations whenever I encountered technical difficulties.

What I highly appreciate is that mentors not only guided me toward solutions but also explained the causes behind problems. This helped me develop the ability to research independently and handle similar situations in the future.

In addition, the team admin provided good support by sharing learning materials, updating program information, and assisting with necessary procedures, making the learning process smoother.

---

## 3. Relevance Between the Program and My Major

The topics covered in FCAJ are highly relevant to my field of study, especially:

- AWS Cloud Services.
- Serverless Architecture.
- Infrastructure as Code with AWS CDK.
- Database and Application Deployment.
- CI/CD Pipeline.

These topics helped me understand how to apply academic knowledge to building and deploying real-world systems on Cloud platforms.

---

## 4. Learning Opportunities and Skill Development

Throughout the program, I gained many valuable technical skills, including:

- Working with AWS services.
- Designing Cloud system architectures.
- Deploying infrastructure using Infrastructure as Code with AWS CDK.
- Working with Serverless services.
- Managing source code and collaborating within a team.

Besides technical knowledge, the program also helped me improve my ability to research documentation, analyze problems, and present technical solutions more effectively.

---

## 5. Culture and Team Spirit

The program provided a positive working environment where everyone supported each other during learning activities and lab exercises.

When encountering errors or challenges, members were willing to discuss and share experiences. This encouraged me to continue exploring new technologies and complete assigned tasks effectively.

---

## 6. Policies and Support for Participants

The program provided sufficient learning materials, practical labs, and necessary support to help participants approach Cloud technologies effectively.

The combination of theory and hands-on practice helped me better understand real-world system deployment instead of only learning concepts.

---

# Feedback Questions

## What did I enjoy most during the program?

The thing I enjoyed most was having the opportunity to directly practice with AWS services and apply the knowledge to build real-world systems.

Through the program, I not only learned how individual services work but also understood how to combine multiple services to create a complete Cloud architecture.

---

## What improvements do I think the program could make?

In my opinion, the program could include more advanced hands-on sessions focusing on topics such as Cloud Security, AWS cost optimization, and large-scale system architecture design.

Additionally, more sharing sessions from experienced Cloud engineers would help participants gain better insights into career development and real-world industry practices.

---

## Would I recommend this program to others?

Yes. I would recommend the FCAJ program to students who want to learn more about Cloud Computing, AWS, and modern technologies.

This program is suitable for students who want to improve their practical knowledge, receive guidance from mentors, and experience a professional system development process.

---

# Suggestions and Expectations

Some suggestions for improving the program in the future:

- Organize more advanced workshops about AWS Architecture, Security, and DevOps.
- Provide more practical labs with higher complexity.
- Increase networking activities among participants.
- Provide additional reference materials after each topic so participants can continue learning independently.

I hope the **First Cloud AI Journey** program will continue to grow in the future and create more opportunities for students to access modern Cloud and AI technologies.
